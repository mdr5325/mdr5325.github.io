About
My name is Michael Ross and I am currently a sophomore at Penn State. I am majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Math. I will be done with all of the courses I need to graduate by the end of my junior year, and plan on taking some graduate level courses before I hopefully move on to graduate school.

In my spare time I like to play golf 2-3 times a week, and I develop apps with three of my friends who I have been programming with since early freshman year. We've attended several hackathons, made a couple of apps (some better than others), and are currently working on our latest app, ez-rider. The prototype for this app was made at HackPSU 2014. If you would like more information on this app, there's a page on this website dedicated to it.

In the foreseeable future, I hope to get a summer internship in the field of software engineering. It's a pretty broad request, but I'd be open (and qualified) to pursue an internship in app development, web development, and really any problem that could be tackled with programming (the languages I am proficient in can be found in my resume on the career page).
